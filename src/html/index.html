Lead Agent Lisa Burrows

Lisa Burrows is deeply rooted in the Eaton Community and Preble County. Her service to the area began in her younger years, under the mentorship of her father, Herb Gibson, the founder of Gibson Insurance, established in 1953. From a young age, Lisa embraced the values of hard work and putting customer needs at the forefront. A proud graduate of Eaton High School (Class of 1983), Lisa embarked on a full-fledged career with Gibson Insurance in 1988. For over three decades, she has worked alongside Herb, who steered the business for an impressive 65 years. In 2019, Lisa took the reins, becoming the lead agent for Gibson-Insurance, ensuring its continued success in the future. With a commitment spanning over 30 years to the community, Lisa represents the heart of Gibson-Insurance. Her extensive experience stands testament to the quality and dedication that clients can expect from a genuinely local agent.
